What Is an Automatic Barn Door System?

An automatic barn door system, also known as a motorized barn door opener or electric sliding barn door system, allows your door to open and close automatically using a compact drive system. Instead of manually sliding your door, the system uses a powered motor to move the door smoothly along its existing track.

SlideDrive™ is designed to upgrade traditional sliding barn doors into fully automated systems without changing the original hardware, making it one of the most efficient retrofit solutions available.

Product Overview

SlideDrive™ is a floor-mounted automatic barn door opener designed to work directly at the existing floor guide location. Instead of replacing your track system or installing bulky overhead motors, the unit uses a powered friction drive wheel to move the door smoothly along its current rail.

Because the drive system stays stationary while the door moves past it, SlideDrive™ maintains a compact footprint, clean installation, and consistent performance across a wide range of door sizes and weights.

For new installs, SlideDrive™ can be paired with a compatible floor guide system. For existing setups, it integrates with many standard barn door guides — allowing you to upgrade your door to a fully automated system without major modifications.

Interior vs Exterior Systems

The system shown on this page represents a standard interior configuration. For exterior applications such as shutters or outdoor sliding systems, each SlideDrive™ unit is custom-built with a modified design to handle weather exposure, heavier door construction, and environmental conditions.

A standard interior unit (with control panel inside the enclosure) is approximately: 7.5” (W) × 5.5” (H) × 5.5” (D)

Depending on the setup, the control panel can either be mounted within the unit enclosure or installed separately (such as inside the home), which can reduce the size of the exterior drive unit and allow for a cleaner installation.

How SlideDrive™ Works

Guide Mounted Drive System

SlideDrive™ connects directly to the barn door floor guide assembly at the base of the doorway, keeping the system compact and aligned with the door’s natural movement path.

Dual Drive System

Depending on the application, SlideDrive™ uses either a precision drive wheel or a rack and pinion system. The wheel system is ideal for standard residential doors, while the rack and pinion setup provides stronger, more controlled movement for larger or heavier doors.

Adjustable Drive Control

The system allows installers to fine-tune pressure and movement based on door weight, size, and resistance — ensuring smooth, consistent operation across different setups.

Stop & Position Control

Adjustable stop triggers ensure the door opens and closes to the correct position every time, preventing over-travel and maintaining alignment.

Does it mount directly to the floor?

The system integrates with the floor guide assembly at the base of the doorway, keeping the installation clean without requiring full floor mounting.

Can it work with my current floor guide?
